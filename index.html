      Testing

      Testing your setup:
      Open rstudio
      Open your text editor
      Open a terminal window:
      - macosx - search "terminal"
      - Windows - click on GitBash
      type "git"
      If you get any message other than "git: command not found", you are set!
      If any of these fail, contact an organizer!
